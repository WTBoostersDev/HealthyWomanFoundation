About Us

Healthy Women, Non-Profit Organization

Healthy Woman Foundation is a non-governmental organization that aims to inform and empower young women and girls about sexual and reproductive health, maternity health, and reproductive conditions that frequently affect women across the African continent.

Founded in 2022 by a team of 12 female experts in the health and technology sector from countries all over Africa, not to mention a few- Nigeria,Uganda,Kenya, Madagascar and Ghana, Healthy Woman Foundation through donors and sponsors has grown to 30 countries in Africa and plans to expand its reach. Through donors ,partnerships and volunteers, we continue to grow and educate and change the life of one woman at a time

Our Mission
  • Educate and empower women and girls about their reproductive health
  • Debunk myths and promote a healthy lifestyle among women
  • Support girls and women in rural areas by donating items such as sanitary pads, free health screenings
